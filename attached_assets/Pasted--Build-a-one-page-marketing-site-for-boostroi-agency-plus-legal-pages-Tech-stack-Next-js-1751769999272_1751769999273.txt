### Legal pages
*Plain, editable HTML in `<main className="prose"> … </main>` blocks.*

- **Terms of Service** – service scope, payment terms, no-guarantee clause, governing law: Western Australia.
- **Privacy Policy** – states data collected (contact info, analytics), storage in Replit DB/Stripe, compliance with Australian Privacy Act 1988.
- **Refund Policy** – 14-day money-back on Starter; Pro & Legendary refundable prorata first 14 days, thereafter month-to-month cancel anytime.
